About Real Oil Project

Welcome to the Real Oil Project. Our mission is to revolutionize the way used engine oil is collected and recycled. We also focus on dust collection and providing timely feedback to our users.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable environment by ensuring that used engine oil is properly collected and recycled. We aim to reduce the environmental impact of oil disposal through innovative solutions.

Our Vision

To be a leading organization in oil recycling and waste management, promoting a cleaner and healthier environment for future generations.

Oil Recycling

We collect and recycle used engine oil, converting it into reusable products to reduce environmental harm.

Dust Collection

Our dust collection systems ensure that particulate matter is effectively captured, promoting cleaner air.

User Feedback

We provide timely feedback to our users about the status of their collected oil, ensuring transparency and efficiency.
